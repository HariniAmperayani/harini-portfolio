Case Study · UX + UI

Frugal — Finance made approachable

A simple budgeting companion designed to guide everyday users, not intimidate them with charts. I led research, defined the problem, prototyped flows in Figma, and ran usability testing with five participants to refine the navigation and confidence-building microcopy.

  • Role: UX Designer · Tools: Figma
  • Focus: Guided learning, actionable tips, progressive goals
  • Outcome: Higher task clarity and navigation confidence during usability tests
